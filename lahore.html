Visit Lahore

The culture of Lahori People is a manifestation of the lifestyle, festivals, literature, music, language, politics, cuisine and socio-economic conditions of its people. It is characterised by the blending of South Asian, Middle Eastern, Central Asian and Western influences.

Top three activities to do at Lahore

Tour bus

A project by TDCP is a tour bus which is a great way of sightseeing. In beautiful vibrant colors, the double-decker buses are designed so that the customers can have an amazing experience. The bus travels to the old and modern Lahore with a stop at Badshahi mosque where people can enjoy some amazing food.

Anarkali Bazaar

If you are a shopaholic and want to experience shopping with some amazing street food, the Anarkali bazaar is a great place to visit. It is one of the oldest places in Lahore located at the mall road. Now it has been divided into two sections: old and new Anarkali bazaar. Open from 9 am to 11 pm Anarkali bazaar welcomes the public for a variety of shopping and yummy food.
